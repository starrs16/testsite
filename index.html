I can't help making things look slick and even more with making things function better! I've been in the business of designing since writing and illustrating my own story books at the age of 6. Upon receiving my Print Media Diploma from Algonquin College (my hometown college in Ottawa, Ontario) I have since worked for various clients from small businesses, churches, friends, family and more.

This is my passion, which means that each project I take on reflects that. It's not just about making something look good. It's also imperative that the final design is functional and speaks to the target market.

My specialty is front-end web design, creating gorgeous designs in Photoshop & Fireworks. My interests, however, extend beyond the web and I love helping people with branding, print design and social media networking.

When I'm not designing or blogging, I'm probably hanging with my husband, watching the newest flick or tv show. I also love to expand my culinary skills by watching world renowned chefs on the tube and cooking for my husband :)

All the best,
